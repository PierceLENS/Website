PierceLENS Cookie Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you browse websites. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

2. How PierceLENS Uses Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience and to understand how our site is being used. Specifically, we use the following types of cookies:

2.1. Strictly Necessary Cookies (Essential)

These cookies are essential for the operation of our website. They enable you to navigate the site and use core features, such as accessing secure areas and maintaining configuration preferences (like the base price on the customization page). Without these cookies, the site cannot function correctly.

2.2. Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use the website, for instance, which pages visitors go to most often, and if they get error messages from web pages. These help us improve how the website works.

2.3. Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your chosen language or region) and provide enhanced, more personal features. For example, they may be used to remember the items you configured in the 'P.L Builder'.

3. Your Choices

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie preferences by clicking the 'Accept' button on the banner or by adjusting your browser controls to refuse cookies.

  • Most browsers allow you to manage your cookie preferences.
  • Please note that if you choose to disable strictly necessary cookies, the website may not function as intended.

4. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time in order to reflect changes to the cookies we use or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please revisit this Cookie Policy regularly to stay informed about our use of cookies.
